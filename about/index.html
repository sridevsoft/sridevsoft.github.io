About Sridevsoft

We craft high-quality mobile applications designed to bring peace, chanting, mindfulness, and spiritual focus into your daily life.

🌸 Our Mission

At Sridevsoft, we believe that technology can be a force for peace. Modern life is filled with persistent noise, notifications, and distractions. Our mission is to create quiet spaces within your digital devices—providing high-quality devotional chants, traditional mantra repetitions, meditation counters, and sleep relaxation utilities that help you quiet the mind and elevate the soul.

🧘 What We Do

We develop mobile applications for Android that focus on spiritual mindfulness. We specialize in recording, processing, and presenting sacred mantras and devotional songs with clear, traditional audio, combined with interactive features that support daily meditation practices.

Our applications serve millions of users seeking spiritual tranquility, offering rich text lyrics in local languages, customizable chanting repeat loops, mala bead counters, and timing control parameters.

🎧 High-Quality Audio

We source and optimize serene audio loops and background ambient sounds to provide a deeply immersive meditation experience.

📿 Interactive Utilities

Features like digital Mala beads, counting targets, and vibration feedback help keep track of your daily mantras without needing physical counters.

🌐 Offline Accessibility

Our apps are designed to work entirely offline, ensuring you can meditate and listen to mantras anywhere without needing network coverage.

✨ Minimalistic UI

We design interfaces that are simple, distraction-free, and respectful of the spiritual nature of the content.

🌿 Our Story

Sridevsoft started with a simple goal: to make authentic spiritual chants easily accessible to anyone, anywhere. What began as a single application dedicated to meditation and chanting has grown into a diverse catalog of over 19 applications on Google Play—including popular releases like Buddha Chants, Ganesh Moola Mantra, Kala Bhairava Ashtakam, and Sleep Relax.

We are continuously refining our apps, improving audio compression so they remain lightweight, updating lyrics, and expanding our support utilities. We are deeply grateful to our community of users who continue to share their spiritual journeys with us.
