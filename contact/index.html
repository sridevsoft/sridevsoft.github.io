App Support FAQ

Do your devotional apps function offline?

Yes. All devotional chant audio, mala counters, and lyrics are embedded inside our applications. Once downloaded, you do not need an internet connection to use the core features of the apps.

How do I request a new mantra or devotional chant?

We welcome community input! Please fill out the contact form or email us at sridevsoft@gmail.com with the specific chant or lyrics you would like us to add, and we will review it for future updates.

Are there any privacy concerns or user tracking?

We respect your privacy. Sridevsoft applications do not collect or store personal data. AdMob serves standard ads to support development, which are subject to Google's standard privacy policies.

How can I report a bug or crash in the app?

Please use the form above or email us. Include your phone model, Android OS version, and a brief description of the steps that lead to the error. This helps us reproduce and patch it quickly.
